Tofu Shakes

Enjoy something both sweet and good for you with these gloriously delicious, sensationally healthy tofu shakes. Tofu is low in calories and full of protein, making it an ideal ingredient for dieters, vegetarians, vegans and health-food lovers alike. These tofu shakes are super easy to rustle up and make a fabulous sweet treat for enjoying in the mornings or afternoons, or even as a healthy dessert.

Ingredients

Chocolate Tofu Smoothie

  • 350g soft silken tofu
  • 125ml soy milk
  • 125g ice
  • 5ml honey
  • 5ml cocoa powder

Vanilla Tofu Smoothie

  • 350g soft silken tofu
  • 125ml soy milk
  • 125g ice
  • 5ml stevia (or other preferred sweetener)
  • 5ml vanilla extract

Strawberry Tofu Smoothie

  • 350g soft silken tofu
  • 125ml soy milk
  • 125g ice
  • 6-8 strawberries

Banana Tofu Smoothie

  • 350g soft silken tofu
  • 125ml banana soy milk
  • 125g ice
  • 1 fresh banana

Steps

  1. Chocolate Tofu Smoothie: Put 350g soft silken tofu, 125ml soy milk, 125g ice, 5ml honey and 5ml cocoa powder into a blender. Blend until smooth and enjoy.
  2. Strawberry Tofu Smoothie: Put 350g soft silken tofu, 125ml strawberry soy milk, 125g ice and 6-8 strawberries into a blender. Blend until smooth and enjoy.
  3. Banana Tofu Smoothie: Put 350g soft silken tofu, 125ml banana soy milk, 125g ice and 1 fresh banana into a blender. Blend until smooth and enjoy.
  4. Vanilla Tofu Smoothie: Put 350g soft silken tofu, 125ml soy milk, 125g ice, 5ml stevia (or other preferred sweetener) and 5ml vanilla extract into a blender. Blend until smooth and enjoy.

Tips!

  • For more interesting depth of flavour, try substituting the ice for a frozen banana.
  • These milkshakes are meant to be an enjoyable sweet treat. So why not try other flavours? Other delicious additions to your milkshake include honey, fresh blueberries, or a shot of espresso coffee.
